Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

Summary

At Linearama, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Your Data

  • Note Storage: All your notes are stored locally on your device.
  • No Collection: We do not collect any personal data or telemetry data.
  • No Server Storage: Your notes are never sent to our servers unless you explicitly enable iCloud synchronization.
  • Internet Access: Linearama may connect to the internet only to check for updates.

iCloud Synchronization

  • If you enable iCloud synchronization, your notes will be stored in your personal iCloud account.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data, as it's managed by Apple's infrastructure.
  • You can disable iCloud synchronization at any time in the Settings menu.

Third-Party Services

  • Linearama includes a link to join our Discord community. Discord has its own privacy policy.
  • We do not share any of your data with third parties.

File Exports

  • You can export your notes to the Files app.
  • These exported files are stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account if enabled.
  • We have no access to these exported files.

Your Control

  • You have complete control over your data.
  • You can delete notes at any time.
  • Deleted notes are automatically purged after one week.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at mimmi.ferrini@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
